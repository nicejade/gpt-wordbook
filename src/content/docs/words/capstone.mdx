### 分析词义

"Capstone"是一个名词，主要有两个含义：
1. 顶石：建筑物顶部的石头
2. 顶点：比喻顶点、最高成就或最终项目

<Pronunciation word="Capstone" uk="/ˈkæp.stəʊn/" us="/ˈkæp.stoʊn/" client:only />

### 列举例句

1.  The capstone project was the final requirement. (顶点项目是最终要求。)
2.  The capstone of the building was impressive. (建筑物的顶石令人印象深刻。)
3.  This achievement was the capstone of his career. (这个成就是他职业生涯的顶点。)

### 词根分析

"Capstone"由 "cap"（顶部）和 "stone"（石头）组成，字面意思是"顶部的石头"。

### 词缀分析

- 词根：cap（顶部）
- 词根：stone（石头）

### 文化背景

"Capstone"常用于描述顶点、最高成就，在教育中常指"capstone project"（顶点项目），在建筑和日常语境中经常使用。

### 单词变形

- 复数形式：capstones

### 记忆辅助

可以将 "capstone" 分解为 "cap"（顶部）和 "stone"（石头），想象"建筑物顶部的石头"。

My senior capstone project was the most challenging thing I did in college - it combined everything I'd learned over four years into one big research project. When I finally finished it, I felt like I had reached the capstone of my education. It was incredibly satisfying.
