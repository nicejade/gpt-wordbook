### 分析词义

"Greens"是名词。The colour of growing foliage, as well as other plant cells containing chlorophyll; the colour between yellow and blue in the visible spectrum; one of the primary additive colour for transmitted light; the colour obtained by subtracting red and blue from white light using cyan and yellow filters.。此外，它还可以表示"Leaves and leaf-like parts of edible plants when eaten as vegetables or in salads."。
