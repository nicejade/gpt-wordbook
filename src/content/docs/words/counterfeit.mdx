### 分析词义

"Counterfeit"是一个形容词或名词，主要有两个含义：
1. 作为形容词：伪造的，假冒的或非真实的
2. 作为名词：假货，伪造的物品

<Pronunciation word="Counterfeit" uk="/ˈkaʊn.tə.fɪt/" us="/ˈkaʊn.t̬ɚ.fɪt/" client:only />

### 列举例句

1.  The counterfeit money was discovered. (假币被发现了。)
2.  They sold counterfeit goods. (他们出售假货。)
3.  The counterfeit watch looked real. (假表看起来很真实。)

### 词根分析

"Counterfeit"来自古法语 "contrefait"，由 "contre-"（反）和 "fait"（制作）组成，字面意思是"反制作"。

### 词缀分析

- 前缀：counter-（反）
- 词根：feit（制作）

### 文化背景

"Counterfeit"常用于描述伪造、假冒的物品，在法律、商业和日常对话中经常使用。

### 单词变形

- 过去式：counterfeited
- 现在分词：counterfeiting
- 复数形式：counterfeits

### 记忆辅助

可以将 "counterfeit" 分解为 "counter-"（反）和 "feit"（制作），想象"反制作、伪造"。

I almost bought a counterfeit designer bag online - it looked real in the photos and was way cheaper than the real thing. But my friend warned me that counterfeit goods are illegal and often poor quality. I'm glad I listened to her and didn't waste my money on a fake.
