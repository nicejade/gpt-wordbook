### 分析词义

"Counterattack"是一个名词或动词，主要有两个含义：
1. 作为名词：反击，对攻击进行反击的行为
2. 作为动词：反击，对攻击进行反击

<Pronunciation word="Counterattack" uk="/ˈkaʊn.tər.ə.tæk/" us="/ˈkaʊn.t̬ɚ.ə.tæk/" client:only />

### 列举例句

1.  The counterattack was successful. (反击是成功的。)
2.  They launched a counterattack. (他们发起了反击。)
3.  The counterattack caught the enemy off guard. (反击让敌人措手不及。)

### 词根分析

"Counterattack"由 "counter-"（反）和 "attack"（攻击）组成，字面意思是"反攻击"。

### 词缀分析

- 前缀：counter-（反）
- 词根：attack（攻击）

### 文化背景

"Counterattack"常用于描述对攻击进行反击的行为，在军事、体育和日常对话中经常使用。

### 单词变形

- 过去式：counterattacked
- 现在分词：counterattacking
- 复数形式：counterattacks

### 记忆辅助

可以将 "counterattack" 分解为 "counter-"（反）和 "attack"（攻击），想象"反击"。

### 助记故事

In the chess game, my opponent made a strong move, but I was ready with a counterattack. I had been planning it for several moves, waiting for the right moment. The counterattack turned the game around and put me in a winning position. Sometimes the best defense is a good offense.
