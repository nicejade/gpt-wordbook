### 分析词义

"Entirely"是一个副词，表示完全地、全部地，用来强调程度，表示完全、全部或彻底。这个词强调完全和全部。

<Pronunciation word="Entirely" uk="/ɪnˈtaɪə.li/" us="/ɪnˈtaɪr.li/" client:only />

### 列举例句

1.  She was entirely correct. (她完全正确。)
2.  The plan was entirely different. (计划完全不同。)
3.  He agreed entirely. (他完全同意。)

### 词根分析

"Entirely"来自形容词 "entire"（完全的），加上后缀 "-ly"（副词后缀）。

### 词缀分析

- 词根：entire（完全的）
- 后缀：-ly（副词后缀）

### 文化背景

"Entirely"常用于强调程度，表示完全、全部，在正式和日常语境中经常使用。

### 单词变形

- 形容词形式：entire（完全的）

### 记忆辅助

可以将 "entirely" 与 "entire"（完全的）联系起来，"-ly" 后缀将其变为副词形式。

### 助记故事

When I thought I had lost my keys, I was entirely wrong - they were in my pocket the whole time. The entirely different approach to the problem worked much better than what we tried before. Using "entirely" emphasizes that something is complete or total, with no exceptions or qualifications.
