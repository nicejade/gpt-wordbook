### 分析词义

"Designedly"是一个副词，表示故意地、有意地，用来描述故意、有意或计划好的行为。这个词强调故意和有意。

<Pronunciation word="Designedly" uk="/dɪˈzaɪ.nɪd.li/" us="/dɪˈzaɪ.nɪd.li/" client:only />

### 列举例句

1.  He designedly ignored the question. (他故意忽略了这个问题。)
2.  The mistake was designedly made. (错误是故意造成的。)
3.  She designedly avoided the topic. (她故意避免了这个话题。)

### 词根分析

"Designedly"来自形容词 "designed"（设计的），加上后缀 "-ly"（副词后缀）。

### 词缀分析

- 词根：designed（设计的）
- 后缀：-ly（副词后缀）

### 文化背景

"Designedly"是正式用词，常用于描述故意、有意的行为，在正式和文学语境中经常使用。

### 单词变形

- 形容词形式：designed（设计的）

### 记忆辅助

可以将 "designedly" 与 "designed"（设计的）联系起来，"-ly" 后缀将其变为副词形式。

When my friend designedly left me out of the party invitation, I was hurt - it wasn't an accident, she did it on purpose. Being designedly excluded feels worse than being forgotten, because it means someone intentionally didn't want you there. Sometimes people act designedly to send a message.
