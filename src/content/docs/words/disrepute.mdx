### 分析词义

**Disrepute** 是一个名词。常用于 **fall into disrepute** 或 **bring something into disrepute**，表示由于不当行为而坏了名声。

<Pronunciation
  word="Disrepute"

  us="/dɪsɹɪˈpjuːt/"
  client:only
/>

### 列举例句

1. The scandal brought the entire profession into disrepute. (丑闻使整个行业名声扫地。)
2. He has fallen into disrepute due to his involvement in the fraud case. (由于参与诈骗案，他已经名声狼藉。)
3. The old theory has long since fallen into disrepute among scientists. (旧理论在科学家中早已名誉扫地。)

### 词根分析

- **前缀**：`dis-` (不/相反)
- **词根**：`-repute-` (名声/认为，如 reputation)
- **含义**：没有好名声。

### 记忆辅助

联想 **reputation** (名誉)。**Dis-** (去掉) + **repute** -> 毁掉名誉。

### 助记故事

The athlete's drug use brought the sport into disrepute and led to several lost sponsorships.
(该运动员服用药物的行为使这项运动名声受损，并导致失去了几项赞助。)
